About D'Plato Database

D'Plato Database is a simple yet functional system designed to compute and manage the sales of a small
local eatery or karinderya. It was developed by second-year college students with the goal of creating an
efficient, user-friendly tool for tracking daily transactions, improving sales transparency, and minimizing
manual errors in the process.
